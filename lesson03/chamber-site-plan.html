Website Planning Document

Site Name

The site is going to be called "Montego Bay Chamber of Commerce," which is hosted on GitHub. This site name was chosen based on the region of Montego Bay, St. James, Jamaica.

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the Montego Bay Chamber of Commerce website is to serve as a hub for the Chamber of Commerce located in the city of Montego Bay, Jamaica.

The Montego Bay Chamber of Commerce is a local organization of businesses in Montego Bay, Jamaica, with two key purposes:

  1. Boosting the Tourism Sector: The website aims to promote and support the thriving tourism industry on the island of Jamaica. It will provide information about local attractions, accommodations, and activities to attract tourists and enhance their experience in Montego Bay.
  2. Promoting Local Businesses: Another essential purpose of this site is to promote and support local businesses. This includes businesses in various sectors, such as hotels, Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) companies, and both small and large-scale enterprises. The website will facilitate networking among local businesses, offer resources for business growth, and highlight success stories to encourage entrepreneurship in the region. Additionally, it will serve as a platform for governmental business support initiatives that contribute to making Jamaica an attractive place to live, grow, and conduct business.

In summary, the Montego Bay Chamber of Commerce website aims to foster economic development in Montego Bay by boosting tourism and supporting local businesses in various sectors, ultimately contributing to the prosperity of the region.
