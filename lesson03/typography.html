Typography

  • Cormorant Garamond: Cormorant Garamond is a classic and elegant serif font that will be used for most of the heading and body text on the website, across various design applications. It has a timeless and sophisticated look, making it suitable for formal documents and websites.
  • Lexend Deca: Lexend Deca is a modern sans-serif font designed for improved readability, especially at smaller text sizes. It's often used for body text in articles, reports, and websites. This font will primarily be used for listed items.
  • Roboto: Roboto is a versatile sans-serif font family developed by Google. When used at a weight of 100, it becomes very thin and lightweight. It can be used for a variety of purposes, including body text, but it may be more suitable for larger headings or titles when set to such a light weight. However, this font will mainly be used in the footer.
