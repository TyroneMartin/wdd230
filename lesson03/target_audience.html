Scenarios

These scenarios are based on questions that would be asked by our site visitors, representing the target audience of the organization or company.

Here is a list of scenarios that can be linked to our Q&A:

  1. A local entrepreneur from Jamaica, visiting the Montego Bay Chamber of Commerce website, asks, "What business events will be coming up in the near future in the Ottawa Area?" This question reflects their interest in networking and staying updated on business-related events in their region.
  2. An individual with extensive education and business experiences queries, "Where can I share my education and business experiences?" This scenario indicates that they are seeking a platform or section on the website where they can contribute their expertise, share insights, or collaborate with other professionals.
  3. A new business owner in Montego Bay asks, "I would like to know how to register my new business with the Chamber of Commerce?" This inquiry demonstrates their need for guidance and information on the process of registering a business with the Chamber, highlighting the website's role as an informational resource.
  4. A prospective investor interested in the Jamaican market wonders, "What useful tips about possible new business opportunities?" This scenario suggests that they are looking for insights, market trends, and tips that can assist them in exploring potential business opportunities within Jamaica, showcasing the website's role in providing valuable market information.
  5. A person intending to develop interests in local companies and businesses in Jamaica inquires, "How can I connect with and support local companies and businesses in Jamaica?" This scenario reflects their desire to engage with the local business community and signifies the website's potential to facilitate connections and promote local businesses.
