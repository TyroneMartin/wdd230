Event: Montego Bay Music Festival

Join us for the annual Montego Bay Music Festival, a three-day extravaganza celebrating the rhythm and soul of Jamaica!

Vibrant event brings together local and international music artists to showcase a diverse range of musical genres, from reggae and dancehall to jazz and world music.

Date: December 15th - 17th,

Location: Montego Bay Harmony Beach Park

Tropical Treasures Boutique

Discover the story behind Tropical Treasures Boutique, a locally-owned gem in Montego Bay. Founded by Lisa-Ann Campbell, this charming boutique specializes in handcrafted jewelry and accessories inspired by the beauty of Jamaica's tropical landscapes. Lisa's passion for crafting unique pieces using local materials shines through in every item she creates. Explore the collection and take home a piece of Jamaica's natural beauty with you.

Montego Bay Culinary Delights

Meet the culinary genius behind Montego Bay Culinary Delights, Chef Marcus James. With a passion for blending traditional Jamaican flavors with a modern twist, Chef Marcus has taken the Montego Bay dining scene by storm. His restaurant offers a delectable fusion of Caribbean cuisine, showcasing locally-sourced ingredients and innovative recipes. Experience the vibrant tastes of Jamaica at Montego Bay Culinary Delights.

Eco-Adventures Jamaica

Embark on an eco-adventure with Eco-Adventures Jamaica, founded by environmental enthusiast Sarah Thompson. This eco-tour company is dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism while providing unforgettable experiences. Explore the lush rainforests, pristine beaches, and hidden waterfalls of Jamaica with expert guides who are passionate about preserving the island's natural beauty. Join Eco-Adventures Jamaica for an eco-conscious journey through paradise.
